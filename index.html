A picture of a sign that says MOBGEN

TO THE WHITE FLOWERS

And the cattle do not see the hunters

Because they go so far to go

To the beginning of the fields.

In the stillness of this sky

Dies and we go to sleep

And walk along the path

That we live together.

Life is like the dead at work,

The sun is gone again.

I am the only one that made

The dead lie down and the dead men

And the children who live in the sun.

I will say that the sea is still

And the hills are silent.

TO THE WHITE FLOWERS

And the cattle do not shake their breath as they stand close

Straight on to the other side of the sea.

And when the white and the crowded die with the rest

Was the stars and the birds in the snow.

A picture of a sign that says MOBGEN

TO THE WHITE FLOWERS

And the barrel's heart is gone –

The black town where every turf is not a stranger,

And the shell is a fountain clock.

“The beating of the tree, is my freedom.

That thing I like is but still the moon is out

With a fine fresh moon to cool your bones.

Before I come and see you in the dark

I have given you a contrary tongue of me.”

“What say you had to say?” she brought the pressure

Of the steamer from the lake.
